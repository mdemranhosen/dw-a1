## How to study this chapter

- Say all numbers aloud, not only in your head.
- Practice digit by digit first, then in short groups.
- For addresses and phone numbers, point with your finger as you speak.
- Do the drills fast, because contact details are usually spoken fast in real life.

## Workbook routine

1. Count slowly.
2. Count again faster.
3. Say the German after seeing only the number or English.
4. Do the role-play like a phone call or office conversation.

| 144 | hundertvierundvierzig [🔊](audio/numbers/144.m4a) | HOON-dert-feer-oont-feer-tsikh | একশ চুয়াল্লিশ |

Quick tips:

- For phone numbers, say each digit separately: `null sieben drei`.
- For house numbers, say it as a normal number: `144` = `hundertvierundvierzig`.
- `dreißig` is the only tens word with `ß`; the rest end in `-zig`.

দ্রুত টিপস:

- ফোন নম্বরে প্রতিটি ডিজিট আলাদা করে বলো: `null sieben drei`।
- বাড়ির নম্বর সাধারণ সংখ্যার মতো বলো: `144` = `hundertvierundvierzig`।
- `dreißig`-এ `ß` থাকে, বাকি tens-এ `-zig` থাকে।

## Speaking workbook

### Quick response drill

Answer immediately in German.

- `Wie alt bist du?` -> `Ich bin ... Jahre alt.`
- `Wie ist deine Telefonnummer?` -> `Meine Nummer ist ...`
- `Wo wohnst du?` -> `Ich wohne in ...`
- `Wie ist Ihr Geburtsdatum?` -> `Mein Geburtsdatum ist ...`
- `Haben Sie eine Adresse?` -> `Ja.` / `Nein.`

### Number sprint

Say these aloud three times each.

- `11, 12, 31`
- `38, 83`
- `0800 7773344`
- `136`
- `144`

### Tiny role-play

A: `Wie heißt du?`
B: `Mein Name ist ...`
A: `Wie alt bist du?`
B: `Ich bin ... Jahre alt.`
A: `Wie ist deine Telefonnummer?`
B: `Meine Nummer ist ...`

A: `Guten Tag. Wie kann ich Ihnen helfen?`
B: `Ich habe ein Problem.`
A: `Wie ist Ihr Geburtsdatum?`
B: `Mein Geburtsdatum ist ...`

## Common mistakes for English speakers

| Mistake | Better German | Bangla note |
|---|---|---|
| `I have 20 years.` | `Ich bin zwanzig Jahre alt.` | বয়সে `haben` না, `sein` ব্যবহার করো |
| `31 = dreißigeins` | `31 = einunddreißig` | German-এ আগে ছোট সংখ্যা, পরে tens |
| saying phone `0` like English `zero` | say `null` | ফোন নম্বরে `0` = `null` |
| using `du` at the police office | use `Sie / Ihr / Ihnen` | office বা formal জায়গায় `Sie` safe |

## 5-minute daily speaking drill

1. Count from 1 to 12.
2. Say `31`, `38`, `83`, `136`, and `144` aloud.
3. Say your age: `Ich bin ... Jahre alt.`
4. Say your phone number digit by digit.
5. Do the short office role-play once formal, once informal.
